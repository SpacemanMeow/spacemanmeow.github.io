Here's how Meow Money Manager solves your biggest bookkeeping pain points in 1 step:

- **Say goodbye to messy records**: Tap the "+" button to record income or expenses in seconds, categorize each transaction with cute icons—no more guessing what that unfamiliar receipt was for.

- **Easily control budgets**: Set total budgets and category budgets, the app automatically reminds you when you're approaching overspending. Track multiple budgets simultaneously and customize budget cycles (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly).

- **Protect privacy and security**: Secure your financial data with fingerprint or password lock—no one else can view your records. We never store any of your data, backup files are directly saved to your iCloud or WebDav, completely under your control.

- **Gain clear financial insights**: Through pie charts, bar charts, or calendar views, clearly understand where your money goes—filter by time range, discover spending trends, and make smarter financial decisions.

- **Automate to save time**: Set up recurring automatic bookkeeping for regular expenses like rent, salary, and bills, letting the app handle the tedious work. Export reports as CSV files for editing or sharing in Excel, while automatically backing up data to avoid loss.
